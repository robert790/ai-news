What changed between 2024 and 2026.

The job market shifted fast. Skills that mattered in 2024 are baseline in 2026; skills that mattered in 2026 will be table stakes in 2027.

  • 2024: "knew ML, fine-tuned a model" → impressive
  • 2026: "fine-tuned a model and deployed it to production with eval harness" → expected
  • What pays now: RAG systems, agents, evaluation, deployment, observability — not pure model work
  • What companies actually hire for: people who can ship a working AI product, not people who can train one
