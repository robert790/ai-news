The minimum viable feed.

Daily · 10 minutes

The morning scan.

• Hacker News front page (news.ycombinator.com) — filter for AI tag. Best signal of what's actually shipping.

• One X/Twitter list: build a list of 20 AI builders/researchers and check it once a day. Suggested names: Andrej Karpathy, Yann LeCun, Simon Willison, Swyx, Robert Scoble, Ethan Mollick.

• Hugging Face daily papers (huggingface.co/papers) — new research, ranked by community votes.

Weekly · 1 hour

The weekly digest.

• Import AI newsletter (importai.net) by Jack Clark — the most thoughtful weekly AI summary.

• The Batch (deeplearning.ai/the-batch) by Andrew Ng — accessible weekly roundup.

• TLDR AI newsletter — quick hits, no fluff.

• r/LocalLLaMA on Reddit — if you care about open models specifically.

Audio · while commuting

Podcasts that earn their airtime.

• Latent Space (swyx + Alessio) — best interviews with AI builders.

• Lex Fridman Podcast — long-form with the people who built the field.

• The AI Daily Brief (Nathaniel Whittemore) — 15-min daily news.

My honest take: most people over-consume. Pick 2 of the above (one daily, one weekly). Stick with them for 6 months. Mastery comes from depth, not breadth.
