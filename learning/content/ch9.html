Pattern to notice: Romania's AI startups cluster around vertical applications (HR, supply chain, cybersecurity, manufacturing) — not foundation models. That's exactly where small teams can win. You're not competing with DeepSeek. You're building for Romanian/Southeast-European businesses that nobody in Silicon Valley cares about.

The broader European context.

Romania sits inside an EU ecosystem that's both behind and interesting:

  • Europe is behind on LLMs. Over 90% of LLM investment happens outside Europe. Of 101 "notable" models in 2024, only 25 came from Europe vs 61 from the US.
  • Europe is ahead on applications. The McKinsey–WEF "AI Era Europe" report identifies Europe's edge in sector-specific, regulation-friendly, reliability-focused AI products — not in frontier models.
  • Europe is starved of capital. EU AI startups raise 5× less than US ones. This means valuations are lower, talent is cheaper, competition is less cutthroat — easier to build quietly.
  • Mistral AI is Europe's flagship (Paris-based, open weights, government and enterprise contracts). Worth tracking as a peer.
